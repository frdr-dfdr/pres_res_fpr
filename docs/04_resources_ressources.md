# Resources

|Resource|Description|
|:-------|:----------|
|[All Known extensions](https://www.digipres.org/formats/extensions/)|A list of extensions and their known formats.|
|[All Known MIME Types](https://www.digipres.org/formats/mime-types/)|Provides a list of MIME Types and the associated format.|
|[DROID](https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/information-management/manage-information/preserving-digital-records/droid/)|A format identification tool. Maintained by [The National Archives of the UK](https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/).|
|[PRONOM](https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/PRONOM/Format/proFormatSearch.aspx?status=new)|Provides a searchable database to find more information about formats. Maintained by [The National Archives of the UK](https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/).|
|[Siegfried](https://www.itforarchivists.com/siegfried/)|A format identification tool. Maintained by [Richard Lehane](https://richardlehane.me/).|
|[Signature development utility 2.0](https://ffdev.info/)|A tool to help develop signature files for submission in PRONOM. Maintained by [Ross Spencer](https://github.com/ross-spencer).|
|[Sustainability of Digital Formats: Planning for Library of Congress Collections](https://www.loc.gov/preservation/digital/formats/)|Includes information about formats by providing detailed format descriptions. Maintained by the [Library of Congress](https://www.loc.gov/preservation/digital/index.html).|
